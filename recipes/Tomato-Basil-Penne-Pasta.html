Tomato Basil Penne Pasta

"This penne pasta is a Mediterranean-style family staple. Use 2 tablespoons olive oil if basil oil is unavailable." â€”Elisa Stamm

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 Ounce) package penne pasta
  • 1 tablespoon basil oil
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pint grape tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup shredded pepper Jack cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesean cheese
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh basil

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. Heat basil oil and olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add tomatoes and cook until they begin to break down, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in pepper Jack cheese, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese. Cook, stirring occasionally, until cheeses are melted and bubbly, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in basil and pasta. Serve immediately.
